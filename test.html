Singapore Stewards , 09/12/2019

SINGAPORE DECEMBER MEETING

FRIDAY 06 DECEMBER 2019         

 

RACE 1 [TF1526]

On Monday, 09 December 2019, Mr R Hatano, B Trainer for Trainer H Takaoka, advised that HERO IN THE WIND (S John) had passed a veterinary examination on Saturday, 07 December 2019, and the Stable will continue its preparation for next Season.

 

RACE 2 [TF1527]

A veterinary examination on Saturday, 07 December 2019 revealed that WINNING MONSTER (App CK Ng) had returned lame off (right) fore. It will be required to pass a 1000m gallop trial and veterinary examination prior to being declared to race again.

 

RACE 3 [TF1528]

On Monday, 09 December 2019, Trainer R Le Grange advised that PINDUS (V Duric) had pulled up well, however, agreed with the rider’s assessment that the gelding would be better suited dropped back in distance.

 

RACE 3 [TF1528]

On Monday, 09 December 2019, Trainer J O’Hara advised that TAX FREE (M Zaki) had pulled up well, and attributed the performance to the gelding having to work too hard in the early stages to take up a forward position.

 

RACE 6 [TF1531]

A veterinary examination on Saturday, 07 December 2019 revealed that SIAM MP (D Moor) had returned lame near (left) fore. It will be required to pass a 1000m gallop trial and veterinary examination prior to being declared to race again.

SINGAPORE DECEMBER MEETING

SUNDAY 08 DECEMBER 2019

 

RACE 5 [TF1560]

 

On Monday, 09 December 2019, Trainer D Logan advised that LEO (A Collett) had pulled up well, and will look to now step the gelding up in distance.

 

RACE 6 [TF1561]

On Monday, 09 December 2019, Trainer HW Tan could offer no explanation for the performance of REVOLUTION (V Duric) and advised that the gelding will only be given light work for a period now to freshen it up.

 

RACE 8 [TF1563]

On Monday, 09 December 2019, Trainer HW Tan advised that WOLF WARRIOR (S John) had pulled up well, and attributed the performance to the step up in class. He will also reassess the gelding’s training schedule in attempt to increase its endurance.

 

RACE 9 [TF1564]

On Monday, 09 December 2019, Trainer M Walker advised that SACRED REBEL (R Woodworth) had pulled up well and was at a loss to explain its performance. He will experiment with the gelding’s gear and may add blinkers for its next start.

 

RACE 10 [TF1565]

A veterinary examination on Monday, 09 December 2019 revealed that OCULIST (S Noh) had returned lame off (right) fore. It will be required to pass a 1000m gallop trial and veterinary examination prior to being declared to race again.

 

RACE 11 [TF1566]

On Monday, 09 December 2019, Trainer M Walker advised that BRIGHT SUN (I Azhar) had pulled up well, however, believed the mare may not have handled the polytrack surface, and will look to place the mare on the turf surface at its next start.
